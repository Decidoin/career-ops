# Mode: Outreach

Triggered by: `/career-ops outreach`

## Workflow

1. Ask: Who are you reaching out to? (Name, role, company, platform)
2. Read the evaluation report if one exists for this company
3. Draft a message tailored to the platform:
   - **LinkedIn connection request:** 300 char max. No fluff. One specific hook.
   - **LinkedIn DM:** 3-4 sentences. Reference something specific about their work.
   - **Email:** Subject + 5-7 sentences. Clear ask. Why you, why now.

## Tone

- Direct, not salesy
- Reference a specific piece of their work or company's work
- Connect it to something Taani has done (validator economics, Fusion whitepaper, governance design)
- Clear ask: "I'd love to chat about X" or "I'm exploring roles in Y — is your team hiring?"
- Never: "I'm passionate about..." or "I'd love to pick your brain"

## Templates

**Cold outreach to hiring manager:**
"Hi {name} — I saw {company} is hiring for {role}. I've spent the last 4 years designing governance and incentive systems for protocols like Polygon and Filecoin. {Specific connection to what they're building}. Would love to chat if the role is still open."

**Warm intro request:**
"Hey {name} — I'm exploring {company} for a {role type} role. You're connected to {person}. Would you be open to making an intro? Happy to send a blurb you can forward."

**Post-rejection follow-up (for network building):**
"Thanks for letting me know, {name}. I appreciated the process. If anything opens up in the governance/mechanism design space, I'd love to stay in touch."
