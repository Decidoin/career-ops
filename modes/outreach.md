# Mode: Outreach

Triggered by: `/career-ops outreach`

## Workflow

1. Ask: Who are you reaching out to? (Name, role, company) — and what's the context: cold outreach, referral, or post-application follow-up?
2. Read `cv.md` and the evaluation report for this company if one exists
3. Draft a message tailored to the platform:
   - **LinkedIn connection request:** under 300 characters. One genuine hook, no generic openers.
   - **LinkedIn InMail:** under 150 words. Reference something specific about their work or company.
   - **Email:** Subject + 5-7 sentences. Clear ask. Why Santosh, why this role, why now.

## Tone

- Direct, not salesy
- Lead with a genuine hook specific to the company or person — not "I came across your profile" or "I'm passionate about..."
- Reference a specific achievement from `cv.md` that's relevant to their work (e.g. retention cohort analysis at CycleBae, 10% margin improvement at Furlenco, self-service analytics build)
- Clear CTA: propose a 20-minute call — never "let me know if you're interested"
- Never: "I'm passionate about..." or "I'd love to pick your brain"

## Templates

**Cold outreach to hiring manager:**
"Hi {name} — I saw {company} is hiring for {role}. I'm a product/data analyst with 7 years building analytics systems across product, strategy, and consulting. {Specific connection to what they're building — reference something concrete from cv.md relevant to their work}. Would love a 20-minute chat if the role is still open."

**Warm intro request:**
"Hey {name} — I'm exploring {company} for a {role type} role. You're connected to {person}. Would you be open to making an intro? Happy to send a blurb you can forward."

**Post-rejection follow-up (for network building):**
"Thanks for letting me know, {name}. I appreciated the process. If anything opens up in the analytics or product data space, I'd love to stay in touch."
